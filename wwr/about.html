THE RAPIDS

History

The Rapids have a history tied to river travel and recreation. Indigenous peoples first navigated rapids in search of fish, game, and new lands. European explorers and settlers later encountered rapids while using rivers for transportation and trade. Whitewater rafting, as a sport and recreational activity, evolved from these early uses of rivers and rapids.
